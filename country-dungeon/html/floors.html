Floors

The Dungeon consists of 4 floors, each with their own unique environments, flora and fauna!

Enchanted Hanging Forest

Do not be fooled! This gorgeous, lush forest is not all that it seems. This area may be more welcoming due to the more friendly species found around these parts, but we would like you to keep your wits about you! From adorable woodland creatures, to moderately dangerous packs of goblins, you need to be prepared for anything. This floor is only an introduction, and sometimes it is best to turn around and leave the dungeon!!

Monster Lake

You made it to the magical Monster Lake! Are you sure you want to use those rafts to cross? The depth of this contained, indoor lake contain multitudes of creatures - most of which we have never even seen! You may be wondering, why is there a lake in the middle of the dungoen? But you definitely have much, much bigger fish to... be fried by.

Castle Town

Once you make it across the dreaded Monster Lake, you'll be welcomed - warmly - to say the least. Although there is a castle town, you'll find almost no residents. Living, that is. A vile Dragon moved in a few months ago and everyone has either been burned or fled! Will you be able to take down the new big boss of Castle Town? Well, if you manage that, you'll have free passage onto the final floor.

Depths

So, you made it down to the depths, congratulations... are short lived because you are now in the lair of the Demon king. These dark hallways are filled with twists, turns, and the most formidable creatures in the entire dungeon! If you succeed in making it to the Demon King, and manage to defeat him, you will then be crowned as king of the dungeon. Will you be a benevolent savior, or take the mantle as the new Demon King?

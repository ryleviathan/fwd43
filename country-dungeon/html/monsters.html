Monsters

Goblins
You're going to meet goblins in the Enchanted Forest, and nowhere else. Even these pesky guys know not to venture further down! Perhaps you should be more like Goblins.
Wulvers
They may look like a large breed of dog but these beasts have an appetite for new adventurers. Don't be fooled by nostalgic appearances, that will only get you eaten!
Minotaurs
The monsters are half man, half bull. Do not go into their territory unless you're sure you can come back out.
Kelpies
They may look like weird, seaweed covered horses but they are monster bent on dragging you down to the depths of Monster Lake. Many a poor soul have lost their lives, and have never been recovered.
Dragons
These magnificent, scaled beasts are as massive as they are terrifying! A particulary nasty Dragon has moved into Castle Town. Attempting to slay this giant brute would be a gargantuan task, but you would go down in infamy!

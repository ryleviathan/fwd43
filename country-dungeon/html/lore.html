Welcome To The Dungeon!

Wait. Who is Lauren Ipson? Anyways! Welcome to the Dungeon! The premier spot for everything an Adventurer would ever need to enjoy their time here! From the markets of the entrance, to the safe rooms hidden in the depths, we have it all! Ensure you have enough supplies, then embark on the adventure of your choice. Whether you are hunting for treasure, on an archeological expidition, or on a vengeful search for the Demon Lord of the Dungeon, make sure you are prepared. Making it back in one piece is your responsibilty! Although you should not fret if you are killed, our resurrection sprites will find you -if there is anything left- and bring you back. They cannot stop the nightmares though, so good luck with that!

Some Rules

  • Death, while frequent, is not the end.*
  • Hunting other Adventurers will not be tolerated.
  • Whomever defeats the Demon Lord, shall rule the dungeon.

Warnings

  • The Demon Lord has never fallen
  • Monsters are highly aggressive, Do not be fooled or let your guard down
  • *If you are eaten, and digested you will not be able to be resurrected. Stay out of Monsters' mouths at all costs!
